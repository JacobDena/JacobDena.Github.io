Photoshop Meme

Jacob Dena Art 74 Portfolio This photo edit was one of my first assignments in Art 74 class. Our class focused on using photoshop and its editing tools to create something of our own. The theme was to create a meme using any pictures we wanted to make. I decided to use a combination of Marvel and DC characters inside our SJSU campus and create a nice caption of how cool it was to use this edit tool for this class. Adding images and cropping them out was a little bit of a challenge but after it was finished and moved the characters in its right position on campus turned out great for the first time. Matrix
