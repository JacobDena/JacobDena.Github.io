Jacob Dena Art 74 Portfolio Hello my name is Jacob Dena I am 24 years old attending SJSU and this is my portfolio for Art 74 where it has 10 of my best work in this class. I am a photography major at SJSU and Art 74 has been a great preparatory class this semester. Since the beginning of this class a few months ago, I didn't know how to use certain programs in Adobe or know how to use coding. Moving forward to the present, I have learned and use my experiences to create certain digital art in my past assignments and wanted to use this portfolio to show my work. Learning coding has been a difficult experience, but after getting the hang of it and finishing a design, it's the best feeling ever. Matrix
